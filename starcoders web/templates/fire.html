FIRE DETECTION


ONE OF THE MAIN CAUSES FOR ACCIDENTS IS UNEXPECTED FIRE.

AN INTIMATION FOR FLAME HAZARD CAN BE GIVEN TO THE DRIVER EVEN TH FIRE OCCURS IN SMALL AMOUNT.

THIS CAN BE IMPLEMENTED USING A FLAME SENSOR.

THE FLAME IS DETECTED BY THE FLAME SENSOR AND AN ALERT IS GIVEN TO THE DRIVER AFTER A CERTAIN THRESHOLD VALUE.
THIS CAN HELP THE DRIVER TO ESCAPE FROM FIRE ACCIDENTS WITH MINOR INJURIES.
