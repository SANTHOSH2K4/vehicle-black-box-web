FACE RECOGNITION


DROWSINESS REFERS TO FEELING MORE SLEEPY THAN NORMAL DURING THE DAY. PEOPLE WHO ARE DROWSY MAY FALL ASLEEP IN WHEN THEY DO NOT WANT TO SHOW AT TIMES WHICH CAN LEAD TO SAFETY CONCERNS.
TO SOLVE THIS DROWSINESS DURING DRIVING CAN BE DETECTED AND ALERTED BY BLACK BOX.

A HP CAMERA(V390) WAS FITTED IN THE TOP OF THE INSIDE REAR VIEW MIRROR,IT ISFOCUSED TOWARDS THE DRIVER'S FACE.

IF HE YAWN OR CLOSES HIS EYES DUE TO DROWSINESS THE BUZZER SENSOR IN THE BLACK BOX STARTS ALERTING THE DRIVER.

TO MAKE IT POSSIBLE A PYTHON CODE WAS RUN IN THE RASPBERRY PI WHICH WAS INSIDE THE BOX AND CONNECTED TO THE HP CAMERA(V 390). IT ALSO HAVE SOME SPECIFIC ALGORITHM TO DETECT THE STATE OF THE DRIVER USING SENSORS,ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE(FACE RECOGNITION) AND RASPBERRY PI.
