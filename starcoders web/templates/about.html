ABOUT US

VEHICLE BLACKBOX IS A DRIVING BEHAVIOR MONITORING DEVICE, DESIGNED TO KEEP A CHECK ON RASH DRIVING, THUS ENSURING SAFETY ON THE ROAD.

A BLACK BOX IN A AIRCRAFT IS A FLIGHT DATA RECORDER THAT MUST BE IN EVERY AIR VEHICLE. ALL FLIGHT INFORMATION IS RECORDED INTO THE BLACK BOX WITH A SPECIFIC ALGORITHM,THIS IDEA GOING TO BE EXECUTED AMONG THE CARS/AUTOMOBILE.

IT ALSO HAVE SOME SPECIFIC ALGORITHM TO DETECT THE STATE OF THE DRIVER USING SENSORS,ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE(FACE RECOGNITION) AND RASPBERRY PI.
