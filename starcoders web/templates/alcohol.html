ALCOHOL DETECTION


Getting behind the wheel of a vehicle car, truck, motorcycle or any other motorized vehicle after consuming alcohol is a serious crime.

Drinking and driving is sometimes called driving under the influence (DUI) or driving while intoxicated (DWI), and involves operating a vehicle with a blood alcohol content (BAC) level of at least 0.08%.

However, even a small amount of alcohol can lead to harmful situations. Some drivers may not even show warning signs of being under the influence, but that doesn't mean it's any less dangerous. Alcohol can be detected using alcohol sensor connected to arduino.
